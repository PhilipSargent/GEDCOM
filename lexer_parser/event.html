GEDCOM 5.5.5 Entry & Event Primitives

{Size=1:90} ENTRY_RECORDING_DATE:= <DATE_VALUE> The date that this event data was entered into the original source document.
{Size=1:15} EVENT_ATTRIBUTE_TYPE:= [ <EVENT_TYPE_INDIVIDUAL> | <EVENT_TYPE_FAMILY> | <ATTRIBUTE_TYPE> ] A code that classifies the principal event or happening that caused the source record entry to be created.
{Size=1:90} EVENT_DESCRIPTOR:= <TEXT> Text describing a particular event pertaining to the individual or family. This value is usually assigned to the EVEN tag.
{Size=3:4} EVENT_TYPE_FAMILY:= [ ANUL | CENS | DIV | DIVF | ENGA | MARB | MARC | MARL | MARR | MARS | RESI | EVEN ] The classification of a family event[cite: 7].
{Size=3:4} EVENT_TYPE_INDIVIDUAL:= [ ADOP | BAPM | BARM | BASM | BIRT | BLES | BURI | CENS | CHR | CHRA | CONF | CREM | DEAT | EMIG | FCOM | GRAD | IMMI | NATU | ORDN | RETI | PROB | WILL | EVEN ] The classification of an individual event[cite: 20].
{Size=1:90} EVENT_OR_FACT_CLASSIFICATION:= <TEXT> Used by the subordinate TYPE tag to further classify the event or attribute (e.g., "Civic Appointments").
