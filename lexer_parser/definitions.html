GEDCOM 5.5.5 Primitive Definitions

<GEDCOM_VERSION_NUMBER>:= [ "5.5.5" ]
GEDCOM 5.5.5 demands that the version number be "5.5.5" and nothing else[cite: 13611].
<VERSION_NUMBER>:= <digit> {<digit>} . <digit> {<digit>} [ . <digit> {<digit>} ]
A version number consists of two or three numbers separated by a dot.
<GEDCOM_FORM>:= [ "LINEAGE-LINKED" ]
The specification defines exactly one form, "LINEAGE-LINKED"[cite: 13943].
<CHARACTER_ENCODING>:= [ "UTF-8" | "UNICODE" ]
"UNICODE" refers to the UTF-16 encoding[cite: 14146, 14282].
<TRANSMISSION_DATE>:= <DAY> <MONTH> <YEAR>
<DAY>:= [ "1" .. "31" ]
Days must not start with leading zeros[cite: 13609, 13628].
<MONTH>:= [ "JAN" | "FEB" | "MAR" | "APR" | "MAY" | "JUN" | "JUL" | "AUG" | "SEP" | "OCT" | "NOV" | "DEC" ]
<YEAR>:= <digit> <digit> <digit> <digit>
GEDCOM 5.5.5 requires four-digit years.
<TIME_VALUE>:= [ hh:mm[:ss[.fs]] ]
24-hour clock time.
<USER_TEXT>:= {<any_char_except_terminator>}
Text entered by the user, which may be continued across lines using CONC and CONT records[cite: 14148, 14149].
<LANGUAGE_ID>:= {<alpha>}
Commonly uses values like "English" or "French".
<ADDRESS_STRUCTURE>:= 1 ADDR <ADDRESS_LINE> [2 CITY <ADDRESS_CITY>] [2 STAE <ADDRESS_STATE>] [2 POST <ADDRESS_POSTAL_CODE>] [2 CTRY <ADDRESS_COUNTRY>] {1 PHON <PHONE_NUMBER>} {1 EMAIL <ADDRESS_EMAIL>} {1 FAX <ADDRESS_FAX>} {1 WWW <ADDRESS_WEB_PAGE>}
<NOTE_STRUCTURE>:= [ n NOTE @<XREF:NOTE>@ | n NOTE <USER_TEXT> {+1 [CONC|CONT] <USER_TEXT>} ]
Notes can be internal text or pointers to a top-level NOTE record[cite: 13891, 13938].
<SOURCE_CITATION>:= [ n SOUR @<XREF:SOUR>@ [+1 PAGE <WHERE_WITHIN_SOURCE>] [+1 EVEN <EVENT_TYPE_CITED_FROM> [+2 ROLE <ROLE_IN_EVENT>] ] [+1 DATA [+2 DATE <ENTRY_RECORDING_DATE>] [+2 TEXT <TEXT_FROM_SOURCE> {+3 [CONC|CONT] <TEXT_FROM_SOURCE>} ] ] {<MULTIMEDIA_LINK>} {<NOTE_STRUCTURE>} | n SOUR <SOURCE_DESCRIPTION> {+1 [CONC|CONT] <SOURCE_DESCRIPTION>} {<NOTE_STRUCTURE>} ]
