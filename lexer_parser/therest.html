GEDCOM 5.5.5 Additional Primitive Definitions

{Size=1:120} NAME_PERSONAL:= <TEXT> A person's name, with the surname (if known) enclosed in slashes (e.g., William Lee /Parry/).
{Size=1:120} NAME_PHONETIC:= <TEXT> A phonetic variation of a name, used for search and sorting.
{Size=1:120} NAME_ROMANISED:= <TEXT> A romanised variation of a name.
{Size=1:90} NAME_PIECE:= <TEXT> A general component of a name.
{Size=1:120} NAME_PIECE_GIVEN:= <TEXT> Given name or earned name (e.g., Mary).
{Size=1:120} NAME_PIECE_SURNAME:= <TEXT> Family name or surname (e.g., Smith).
{Size=1:60} ADDRESS_CITY:= <TEXT> The name of the city used in the address.
{Size=1:10} ADDRESS_POSTAL_CODE:= <TEXT> The ZIP or postal code.
{Size=1:2048} ADDRESS_WEB_PAGE:= <TEXT> The World Wide Web address (URL).
{Size=1:1} SEX_VALUE:= [ M | F | X | U ] A code for the sex of an individual: M=Male, F=Female, X=Other, U=Unknown.
{Size=1:13} AGE_AT_EVENT:= [ < | > | CHILD | INFANT | STILLBORN ] + [ YY | MM | DD ] A code indicating the age of the person at the time of the event (e.g., 25y 6m 3d).
{Size=1:30} NATIONAL_ID_NUMBER:= <TEXT> A nationally assigned identifier (e.g., a passport number).
{Size=5:8} PLACE_LATITUDE:= [ N | S ] + <DEGREE> The latitude coordinate of a place (e.g., N45.1234).
{Size=5:9} PLACE_LONGITUDE:= [ E | W ] + <DEGREE> The longitude coordinate of a place (e.g., W122.5678).
{Size=5:8} CHARACTER_SET:= [ UTF-8 ] In GEDCOM 5.5.5, the only legal value is UTF-8.
{Size=6:7} RESTRICTION_NOTICE:= [ confidential | locked | privacy ] Specifies the level of confidentiality for the associated data.
{Size=1:15} ROLE_IN_EVENT:= <TEXT> The role played by the person in an event (e.g., CHIL, HUSB, WIFE, WITN, BURI).
