GEDCOM 5.5.5 Month Primitives

{Size=3:3} MONTH:= [ JAN | FEB | MAR | APR | MAY | JUN | JUL | AUG | SEP | OCT | NOV | DEC ] Standard abbreviations for months in the Gregorian and Julian calendars. All values must be exactly 3 characters.
{Size=3:3} MONTH_HEBR:= [ TSH | CSH | KSL | TVT | SHV | ADR | ADS | NSN | IYR | SVN | TAM | AAV | ELL ] Hebrew calendar months. ADR is used for Adar I (or Adar in non-leap years); ADS is used for Adar II.
{Size=4:4} MONTH_FREN:= [ VEND | BRUM | FRIM | NIVO | PLUV | VENT | GERM | FLOR | PRAI | MESS | THER | FRUC | COMP ] French Revolutionary calendar months. All values must be exactly 4 characters. COMP refers to the "Jour compl√©mentaires".
